I'm proficient in Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, and Figma, and I have a strong foundation in typography, layout, and visual hierarchy.

For Ligonier's Global Publishing work, I also want to share something personal. I am Armenian, and as an Armenian I am deeply proud that Armenia was the first nation in the world to adopt Christianity as its national religion, in 301 AD. That heritage is not history to me. It is lived. I am a member of St. Leon Armenian Cathedral in Burbank, and my cousins serve as members of the choir. I am fully fluent in Armenian, and I understand firsthand how important it is for theological resources to feel truly at home in the communities they are meant to reach, not just translated, but adapted with genuine cultural care and reverence.
