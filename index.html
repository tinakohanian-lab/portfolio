About me

Bio

Tina Ohanian is a presentation designer focused on pitch decks, visual storytelling, and branded sales materials within sports and entertainment. Her work centers on creating polished, executive-ready presentations that translate ideas, scripts, and concepts into clear visual narratives. With a background in marketing and partnerships, she brings both strategic thinking and strong layout sensibility to every project.

My approach

My approach to design begins with understanding the story behind each project — its tone, audience, and creative intent. I focus on building clean visual systems, strong typography, and cohesive layouts that support narrative rather than distract from it. Collaboration and iteration are central to my process, and I value feedback as a tool to refine and elevate the final presentation. Whether preparing a pitch deck or sales presentation, my goal is to create materials that feel polished, purposeful, and visually aligned with the project’s identity.
